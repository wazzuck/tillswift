Payment Gateway? 

Simple way to think about it

When someone pays online, there are a few moving parts:

Payment gateway → captures and securely sends the card details
Acquirer (merchant bank) → actually processes the payment and gets you the money
Card networks (Visa/Mastercard) → move money between banks

The payment gateway fee is what you pay for step 1.

What the gateway actually does

A payment gateway:

Encrypts the customer’s card details
Sends them to the relevant bank/card network
Returns an approval or decline in seconds

It’s basically the online equivalent of a card machine at a till.
