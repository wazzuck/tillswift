Why are some providers so much cheaper?

The right answer
changes by business.
The maths decides - not the advertising budget.

The lowest sticker rate is not the lowest real cost. Convenience retail lives or dies on rate, but cafés need free POS, restaurants need same-day settlement and kitchen tools, and online stores need storefront economics. Below: a different winner for each model, based on what keeps more money in your business.

What they're selling you

  • Brand-name reassurance — same rates as similar advertised brands
  • "Flat" pricing that bills debit-only shops at the credit-card rate
  • 12-month merchant agreements that lock you in even when better offers appear
  • Surcharges of +1.5-2.0% on premium and non-UK cards, hidden in the small print

What actually wins

  • Convenience / high-volume retail · Lopay 0.79–0.99% — the rate is the whole game
  • Cafés · Square Free — POS, menu, online orders, loyalty all bundled
  • Restaurants · Dojo — same-day 7am settlement (incl. weekends), tables, KDS, bookings
  • Online retail · Shopify Basic — 1.7% with no flat fee + a full storefront for £25/mo
